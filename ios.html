Set up InSave on iPhone

You're in an in-app browser. Open this page in Safari first — tap the ••• menu → Open in Safari — then continue.

1. Install for reminders

In Safari, tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen → Add. Open InSave from the new home-screen icon.

2. Turn on reminders

Open InSave from the home screen, then tap Enable reminders and allow notifications.

3. Save reels — the no-account way

Paste a link (works today, no login): in Instagram, open a reel → Share → Copy link. Then in the InSave home-screen app, tap Paste a reel link. It's saved in the app and reminders work right away. This is the simplest path — no account needed.

4. Want the one-tap Shortcut? Sign in on both places

The Shortcut is the slick one-tap way to save — but it opens links in Safari, which on iPhone is a separate storage space from your installed home-screen app. So a Shortcut save lands under "Safari-you" and won't show in the app on its own.

The fix is an account. Sign in with Google in both places — the installed InSave app and Safari. That links the two into one library, so reels the Shortcut captures in Safari reconcile into your account and appear in the app with reminders.

Signing in on just one place isn't enough — it has to be both. If that's too fiddly, the paste-a-link method above needs no account at all.
